<p align="justify" style="font-size:16px;font-weight: 500">
ASRS 2025 marks the 14th Annual Science Research Session organized by the Faculty of Applied Sciences, South Eastern University of Sri Lanka (SEUSL). As a multidisciplinary research conference, ASRS 2025 serves as a vibrant platform that brings together leading scientists, researchers, academics, and aspiring research students from across the country. It provides an excellent opportunity to share innovative findings, exchange ideas, and foster collaborations across diverse fields of science.
This year’s proceedings will feature a compilation of peer-reviewed abstracts from participating contributors. Additionally, selected abstracts will be invited to submit full-length articles for publication in the Journal of Science (JSC), offering further recognition and visibility to outstanding research.
